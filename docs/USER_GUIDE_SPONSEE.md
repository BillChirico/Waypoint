## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Download the App**: Available on iOS, Android, and web
2. **Sign Up**:
   - Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
   - Enter your email and create a secure password
   - Or use "Sign in with Google" for faster registration
3. **Complete Onboarding**:
   - Select your role as "Sponsee" (or "Both" if you also sponsor others)
   - Set your sobriety date - this is your original sobriety start date
   - Optionally add a bio to share with your sponsor

### Connecting with a Sponsor

After creating your account, you'll need to connect with a sponsor:

1. **Receive an Invite Code**: Ask your sponsor for their unique invite code
2. **Enter the Code**: On the home screen, tap "Connect with Sponsor"
3. **Submit**: Enter the code and confirm
4. **Wait for Approval**: Your sponsor will receive a notification and can accept the relationship

## Home Dashboard

The home screen is your central hub, displaying:

- **Current Step**: Shows which step you're currently working on (1-12)
- **Sobriety Counter**: Days since your sobriety date
- **Upcoming Tasks**: Quick view of pending tasks from your sponsor
- **Recent Notifications**: Important updates and messages
- **Quick Actions**:
  - View all tasks
  - Message your sponsor
  - Track your progress

## Working with Tasks

Tasks are assignments from your sponsor to help you work through the recovery steps.

### Viewing Tasks

1. **Navigate to Tasks Tab**: Tap the "Tasks" icon in the bottom navigation
2. **View All Tasks**: See pending, in-progress, and completed tasks
3. **Filter by Status**:
   - **Pending**: Not yet started
   - **In Progress**: Currently working on
   - **Completed**: Finished tasks

### Managing a Task

#### Starting a Task

1. Tap on a pending task to view details
2. Read the title, description, and any notes from your sponsor
3. Check the due date
4. Tap "Start Task" to mark it as in-progress

#### Completing a Task

1. Open the in-progress task
2. Complete the required work (reflection, writing, etc.)
3. Tap "Mark Complete"
4. Your sponsor will be notified of completion

### Task Details

Each task includes:

- **Title**: Brief description of the task
- **Description**: Detailed instructions from your sponsor
- **Step Number**: Which of the recovery steps this task relates to
- **Due Date**: When the task should be completed
- **Status**: Current state (pending/in-progress/completed)

## Exploring the Recovery Steps

The Steps tab provides content and guidance for each of the recovery steps.

### Navigating Steps

1. **Go to Steps Tab**: Tap "Steps" in the bottom navigation
2. **View Step Content**: Each step includes:
   - Step title and number
   - Full step description
   - Reflection prompts to guide your thinking
3. **Track Progress**: See which steps you've completed

### Working Through a Step

1. **Read the Content**: Carefully read the step description
2. **Reflect**: Use the provided prompts to journal or meditate
3. **Complete Tasks**: Work on sponsor-assigned tasks related to this step
4. **Discuss with Sponsor**: Use messages to ask questions or share insights
5. **Mark Progress**: When ready to move to the next step, your sponsor can update your current step

## Tracking Your Journey

The Journey tab helps you visualize your recovery progress.

### Features

- **Sobriety Timeline**: Visual representation of your recovery journey
- **Step Progress**: See how far you've advanced through the recovery steps
- **Milestone Celebrations**: Recognition for important dates and achievements
- **Personal Notes**: Private space to journal your thoughts and reflections

### Handling Slip-Ups

Recovery is a journey with challenges. If you experience a slip-up:

1. **Be Honest**: This is a judgment-free tool to support your recovery
2. **Log the Slip-Up**: In your profile, you can privately record a slip-up
3. **Set New Recovery Date**: Update your recovery restart date
4. **Reach Out**: Message your sponsor for support
5. **Keep Going**: Your progress on the steps remains - continue your journey

**Note**: Slip-up data is strictly private and only visible to you.

## Communicating with Your Sponsor

### Sending Messages

1. **Navigate to Messages Tab**: Tap the "Messages" icon
2. **View Conversation**: All messages with your sponsor in one thread
3. **Send a Message**:
   - Tap the message input field
   - Type your message
   - Tap send
4. **Check Read Status**: See when your sponsor has read your messages

### When to Message Your Sponsor

- Questions about tasks or steps
- Sharing insights or breakthroughs
- Requesting support during difficult times
- Scheduling meetings or check-ins
- Celebrating successes and milestones

### Message Best Practices

- **Be Honest**: Share openly about your struggles and successes
- **Be Respectful**: Remember your sponsor is volunteering their time
- **Be Timely**: Respond to messages when you can
- **Be Specific**: When asking questions, provide context

## Managing Your Profile

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Tap "Profile" in the bottom navigation
2. View your profile information and settings

### Profile Settings

#### Personal Information

- **Name**: Update your display name
- **Bio**: Share information about yourself with your sponsor
- **Sobriety Date**: Your original sobriety start date
- **Current Step**: Updated by your sponsor as you progress

#### Account Settings

- **Email**: Change your email address
- **Password**: Update your password
- **Theme**: Switch between light, dark, or system theme

#### Notification Preferences

Control which notifications you receive:

- **Task Assignments**: When your sponsor assigns new tasks
- **Messages**: When you receive messages from your sponsor
- **Step Milestones**: When you complete a step
- **Reminders**: For upcoming task due dates

#### Privacy

- **Slip-Up History**: View your private slip-up log (only visible to you)
- **Account Security**: Enable two-factor authentication (coming soon)

### Disconnecting from a Sponsor

If you need to change sponsors:

1. Go to Profile
2. Tap "Sponsor Relationships"
3. Select your current sponsor
4. Tap "End Relationship"
5. Confirm your choice
6. You can then connect with a new sponsor using their invite code

## Understanding Notifications

### Notification Types

You'll receive notifications for:

- **Task Assigned**: Your sponsor has given you a new task
- **Message Received**: New message from your sponsor
- **Relationship Request**: If you're also a sponsor, when someone wants to connect
- **Step Completed**: Confirmation when you finish a step
- **Task Due Soon**: Reminders for upcoming task deadlines

### Managing Notifications

1. Tap on a notification to view the related item
2. Mark notifications as read by opening them
3. Adjust notification preferences in your profile settings

## Tips for Success

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I have multiple sponsors?

Currently, the app supports one active sponsor relationship at a time. If you need to change sponsors, you can end your current relationship and connect with a new sponsor.

### What if I miss a task deadline?

Don't worry! Task deadlines are guidelines to help you stay on track. Talk to your sponsor about extending the deadline or adjusting the task.

### Is my information private?

Yes. Your profile is only visible to you and your sponsor. Slip-up data is strictly private and never shared. Messages are only visible to you and your sponsor. All data is encrypted and secured.

### Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection to sync data and send messages. However, you can view previously loaded content offline.

### How do I change my sobriety date?

Go to Profile > Edit Profile, and update your sobriety date. This should reflect your original sobriety start date. If you experience a slip-up, you can log it separately in the Journey tab.

### What if I don't have a smartphone?

The app works on web browsers! Visit the web version on any computer with internet access.
